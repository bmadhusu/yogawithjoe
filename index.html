About Joe

Joe is a longtime yoga teacher focused on helping students build mobility, strength, and confidence without forcing the body. Gentle yoga is where most people thrive — it is smart, precise, and deeply restorative.

His classes prioritize options, prop support, and slow transitions. You will always have a way into the pose that feels right for your body.
